# How to get billing setup

Before you can start accepting payments through Helium, you need to configure billing for your app.

## Prerequisites

- An active Helium account
- Access to your app store developer accounts (Apple App Store Connect and/or Google Play Console)

## Apple App Store setup

1. Configure your app's in-app purchases in App Store Connect
2. Create subscription groups and products
3. Connect your App Store Connect account to Helium
4. Verify that products are syncing correctly

## Google Play Store setup

1. Set up your app's billing in Google Play Console
2. Create subscription products
3. Connect your Google Play Console account to Helium
4. Verify that products are syncing correctly

## Testing billing

Use sandbox environments to test your billing setup before going live:

- For iOS, use StoreKit testing or sandbox accounts
- For Android, use Google Play's test tracks and license testing

## Next steps

Once billing is configured, you can start creating paywalls and implementing subscription logic in your app.
