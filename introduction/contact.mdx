# Who you can contact

Need help with Helium? Here are the ways to get in touch with our team.

## Support channels

### Email support

For technical questions, billing inquiries, or general support, email us at:

**support@tryhelium.com**

### Community

Join our community to connect with other Helium developers, share best practices, and get answers to common questions.

### Documentation

Browse our comprehensive documentation for guides, API references, and troubleshooting tips.

## Response times

- **Critical issues**: We aim to respond within 4 hours during business hours
- **General inquiries**: We typically respond within 24 hours
- **Feature requests**: We review and respond within 2-3 business days

## What to include

When contacting support, please include:

- Your app name or Helium account ID
- Platform (iOS, Android, React Native, Flutter)
- SDK version
- Detailed description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Relevant error messages or logs
