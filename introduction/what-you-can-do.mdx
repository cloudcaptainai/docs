# What you can do

Helium provides a comprehensive platform for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Here's what you can accomplish:

## Paywall management

Create and customize paywalls to present subscription offers to your users. Control when and how paywalls appear using triggers and workflows.

## Revenue optimization

Run experiments to test different pricing strategies, paywall designs, and messaging. Track revenue and conversion metrics to optimize your monetization strategy.

## Audience targeting

Segment your users into audiences and deliver personalized experiences based on user behavior, demographics, or custom attributes.

## Analytics and insights

Monitor subscription performance with built-in analytics or integrate with third-party analytics platforms to get a complete view of your business.

## Cross-platform support

Implement subscriptions across iOS, Android, React Native, and Flutter with consistent APIs and behavior.
